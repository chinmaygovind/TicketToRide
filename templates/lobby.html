HOW TO PLAY
  • On your turn: draw 2 train cards, claim a route, or draw destination tickets.
  • Claim routes by playing matching colored cards equal to route length.
  • Locomotives are wild cards — usable as any color.
  • Complete destination tickets to earn bonus points; incomplete ones cost points.
  • Game ends when a player has ≤2 trains left. Everyone gets one final turn.
  • 10-point bonus for the player with the Longest Continuous Path.
