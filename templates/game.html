How to Play

ON YOUR TURN
Draw 2 cards face-up or blind; locomotives are wild
Claim a route play matching cards equal to route length
Draw 3 tickets keep ≥2, return the rest
{% if map_variant == 'europe' %}
SPECIAL RULES
Tunnels
Draw 3 blind cards. Pay 1 extra per matching card drawn — or forfeit the attempt.
Ferries
Segments with a ship icon require locomotives. The number shown is how many.
Stations
Place on a city to borrow one opponent route there for tickets. Costs 1/2/3 trains. Unused stations score +4 pts each.
Starting tickets
Choose ≥2 from 1 long route + 3 short routes dealt at the start.
